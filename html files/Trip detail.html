About the Trip

Imagine you're surrounded by the world's highest peaks around you and everything seems so pure and heavenly, that's what Everest base camp is. For decades, many adventurers have been drawn towards the natural beauty of the highest peak in the world, Mount Everest (8,848.68m/29,029 ft). This adventurous trek is best suited for those whose dream soars higher than the clouds. It is one of the best-traveled destinations of the mountains due to its natural beauty along with calm, charming, and attractive features. Come and join this exhilarating once in a lifetime trekking experience. Once you taste the cold breeze of Everest base camp, you'll remain nostalgic about it your entire life.

Day to Day Itenary


Day 1
Kathmandu

Meet Your CEO and Group Catch the mountain flight to Lukla, enjoying stunning views of the Himalayas. Meet the Sherpas and begin the trek to Phakding.

Day 2
Phakding/Namche Bazaar

Hike to Namche Bazaar, gateway to Everest Base Camp and spend two nights acclimatizing for trek.

Day 3
Namche Bazaar

An acclimatisation day allows for an excursion to Thame and Khumjung, the green village.

Day 4
Namche Bazaar/Thyāngboche

Set out on a 6–7 hr trek through woods and rhododendron forests before arriving in Tengboche.

Day 5
Thyāngboche/Dingboche

Trek to Dingboche, crossing bridges and rivers with sweeping views of the Himalayan region. Spend 2 nights here.

Day 6
Dingboche/Lobuche

Hike through alpine meadows and summer yak pastures towards the end of the moraine of the Khumbu Glacier to Lobuche, which has spectacular views of Nuptse.

Day 7
Everest Base Camp

Today includes a challenging climb past the Khumbu Glacier, arriving in Nuptse. Cross the Changri Glacier to Gorakshep, and then enjoy the highlight of reaching Everest Base Camp.

Day 8
Pheriche/Kyangjuma

Descend through Pangboche, with its great gompa, to Kyangjuma.

Day 9
Kyangjuma/Monjo

An acclimatisation day allows for an excursion to Thame and Khumjung, the green village.

Day 10
Monjo/Lukla

The final day of our trek takes us back to Lukla, where we have a chance to relax after the rigorous adventure we just undertook.

Day 11
Lukla/Kathmandu

Catch the short flight back to Kathmandu, where the rest of the day is free for shopping, sightseeing, or relaxing in one of the many rooftop cafés.
